Follow the steps below to get started using WP Photo Gallery. Your Galleries will be ready in just few minutes!

Creating Your First Gallery

Downloads → Add New

The Downloads menu is your access point for all aspects of your Easy Digital Downloads product creation and setup. To create your first product, simply click Add New and then fill out the product details.

Download Files

Uploading the downloadable files is simple. Click Upload File in the Download Files section and choose your download file. To add more than one file, simply click the Add New button.
