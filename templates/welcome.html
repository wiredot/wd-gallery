Welcome to WP Photo Gallery

Thank you for installing WP Photo Gallery.

Getting Started Support Credits

Follow the steps below to get started using WP Photo Gallery. Your Galleries will be ready in just few minutes!

Creating Your First Gallery

Photo Gallery → Add New Photo Gallery

On this page you can create your first Photo Gallery. Simply enter the Gallery title and click on the Add Photos button. You can chose photos from your WordPress Media Library or upload them. Then you can add a Gallery Cover if you want and Publish the Gallery.

Adding a Gallery List to a Page

{{ __("On any page click on the Add Photo Gallery button directly above the Editor. Then click on the Insert Shortcode button next to Galleries List. Save the page and you're done.", "wp-photo-gallery") }}

{{ __("Shortcode", "wp-photo-gallery") }}:

[wp-photo-gallery]

Adding a Gallery to a Page

{{ __("You can display a single Gallery on a page. Click on the Add Photo Gallery button directly above the Editor. Then click on the Insert Shortcode button next to the Gallery you want to show. Save the page and that's it.", "wp-photo-gallery") }}

{{ __("Example Shortcode (id is a post id of your Gallery)", "wp-photo-gallery") }}:

[wp-photo-gallery id=1]
